Psychic / Dark
 Leveltenmafia is a dual-type Psychic/Dark Pokémon known for its ability to manipulate both the minds and shadows of its opponents. It evolves from its previous form, Mindshroud, after being exposed to a Dusk Stone. Often considered a trickster in the Pokémon world, Leveltenmafia uses its powers to confuse and outsmart other Pokémon and trainers alike.
