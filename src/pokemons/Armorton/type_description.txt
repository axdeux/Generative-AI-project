Steel
 Armorton is a Steel-type Pokémon that resembles a heavily armored turtle. It is not known to evolve from or into any other Pokémon. Armorton's thick steel armor makes it extremely durable in battle, allowing it to withstand heavy attacks. It is often found in industrial areas where it uses its strong shell to help in construction and demolition tasks.
