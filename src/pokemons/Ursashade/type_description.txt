Normal / Dark
 Ursashade is a dual-type Normal and Dark Pokémon that resembles a shadowy bear. It is known for its elusive nature and ability to blend into dark environments seamlessly. Ursashade does not evolve from or into any other Pokémon, making it unique in its standalone form. It is often found in dense, dark forests where it uses its dark type abilities to camouflage and hunt.
