Fire / Flying
 Solflara is a dual Fire and Flying-type Pokémon resembling a small, vibrant bird. It is known for its ability to harness the power of the sun, using solar energy to fuel its fiery attacks. Solflara does not evolve but can increase its power during sunny weather, making it a formidable opponent in battle.
