Ghost
 Ghoulis is a Ghost-type Pokémon that thrives in the shadows of urban environments. It is known for its ability to blend seamlessly into darkness and is often found haunting abandoned buildings and dimly lit streets. Ghoulis can evolve into Spectrify, gaining an eerie glow and more potent haunting abilities.
