Fire / Dragon
 Salomonster is a dual Fire and Dragon-type Pokémon, embodying the ferocity and mythical essence of dragons along with the blazing fury of fire. It is not known to evolve from or into any other Pokémon, making it a unique and standalone creature in the Pokémon world. Salomonster is highly revered for its ability to generate and control flames that can incinerate anything in its path.
