Fire / Dragon
 Salomonster, a dual Fire and Dragon-type Pokémon, is considered a formidable creature in the Pokémon world. It evolves from a smaller, less intimidating form called Emberling. Salomonster is known for its fierce battle prowess and the ability to breathe fire hot enough to melt rocks. It is often sought by trainers who specialize in Dragon-type Pokémon for its strength and majesty.
