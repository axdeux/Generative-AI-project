Water / Ground
 Aquadent is a Water and Ground-type Pokémon that resembles a robust beaver. It is known for its exceptional ability to manipulate both mud and water to create intricate dams and shelters. Aquadent does not evolve but instead focuses on increasing its skills and expanding its habitat. It plays a crucial role in its ecosystem by maintaining water levels and providing habitats for other Pokémon.
