Electric / Dark
 Elgato is a dual-type Electric/Dark Pokémon, resembling a sleek and mysterious cat. It has the ability to generate electricity from its fur and manipulate shadows to confuse opponents. Elgato is known for its stealth and agility, making it a formidable Pokémon in battles. It evolves from Sparkitten and can evolve into Panthunder.
